Founders and Developers (10%)
This allocation is designated for the two founders and any developers who contributed during the initial phase. Tokens from this wallet will be the only ones accessible to the team for personal sales. We commit to allocating 30% of the proceeds from every sale made from this wallet for buybacks. The process will work as follows: whenever a sale is made from the "Founders and Developers" wallet, 30% of the amount will be automatically directed to a separate wallet named "Founders Buyback", which will be used exclusively for repurchasing tokens on the market. Buybacks will be executed at an appropriate time, based on discussions with the community — especially with holders of our token — to ensure alignment with the long-term vision of the project.
Airdrops and Community (20%)
This allocation is dedicated to rewarding and engaging the community through airdrops, giveaways, and incentive programs. The goal is to foster adoption, reward active members, and strengthen the Bear Rich ecosystem.
Liquidity (DEX + CEX) (50%)
Ensuring sufficient market liquidity is crucial for a healthy token economy. This allocation is dedicated to providing liquidity on both decentralized (DEX) and centralized exchanges (CEX), ensuring smooth trading experiences and reducing price volatility.
Treasury and Development (10%)
These funds will be used to support the long-term development and sustainability of the project. This includes funding future upgrades, partnerships, marketing efforts, and operational expenses to drive innovation and adoption.
Future Burn (10%)
The tokens in this wallet are allocated for burns or, depending on market demand, may be transferred to a DEX/CEX liquidity wallet. Any decisions regarding these tokens will be discussed with the community before action is taken. Under no circumstances will this allocation be sold by the founders or any other team members—we will never ‘rug pull’ our community.
